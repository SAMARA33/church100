Ministry

Our mission at Hope Community SDA Church is to have a mantle of intercession that brings forth great moves of God as we pray, disciple and evangelize to build up the Kingdom of God. The goal is to be divinely enabled to consistently pray on behalf of and for others, seeing frequent and specific results. Our sole purpose in this ministry is to stand in the gap with prayer. “And the prayer of faith will save the sick, and the Lord will raise him up. And if he has committed sins, he will be forgiven.” James 5:15.
