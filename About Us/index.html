About Us

our mission

The mission of Hope Community SDA Church is to win souls for Christ by teaching the love of Jesus and reaching out and meeting the needs of the community, by proclaiming the three angels message and the soon coming of Jesus Christ; and by being a lighthouse and a friendly place of spiritual refuge in the community.

About Seventh-day Adventists

The Seventh-day Adventist Church is a mainstream Protestant church with approximately 19 million members worldwide, including more than one million members in North America.

Find Out More

Our Goal

To make the Hope Community SDA Church a place of refuge that will meet the needs of our community and our church family. We want everyone to know that we are commissioned to be a witness for Christ and do all that we can to help families from all walks of life.
